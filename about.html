Michaela Fern Roath was born in Spokane, WA and grew up near the small city of Deer Park, WA. Fern has always been creating since a young age. In high school she started expanding her skills towards the digital and started exploring what she thought was graphic design. She continued her love for illustration and Design in 2012 at Cornish College of the Arts as a Visual Communications student. Four years later she has discovered her love for product packaging, photography, poster design, fashion design and much more. Fern has an incredible sweet tooth, likes to cook to relax and can usually be found sketching something in her personal sketchbook.
